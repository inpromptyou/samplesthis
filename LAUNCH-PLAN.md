# Flinchify Launch Plan — 100 Sign-ups in 30 Days
# Budget: $0 | Start: April 1, 2026

## Pre-Launch (Now → March 31)
- [ ] Graphics/visuals for homepage (current task)
- [ ] Seed 3-5 real test jobs so the marketplace doesn't look empty
- [ ] Add Vercel Analytics (free, takes 2 min)
- [ ] Get 2-3 friends to sign up as testers (social proof)
- [ ] Prep Product Hunt listing (screenshots, tagline, maker comment)

## Week 1: Soft Launch (April 1-7)
**Goal: 25 sign-ups (15 testers + 10 founders)**

- [ ] Post build story on Indie Hackers (not an ad, your story)
- [ ] Post on r/SideProject — "I'm 18, built a testing marketplace"
- [ ] DM 10 founders/day on X who just shipped something
  - Hook: "Hey, I built a marketplace where real people test your app for $5-12. Want $30 free credit to try it?"
- [ ] Post tester recruitment on r/beermoney + r/WorkOnline
  - Hook: "Get paid $10 to test apps for 15 minutes"
- [ ] One TikTok/Reel: "I'm 18 and built a platform where you get paid to test apps"

## Week 2: Product Hunt (April 8-14)
**Goal: 25 more sign-ups (cumulative 50)**

- [ ] Launch on Product Hunt (Tuesday or Wednesday morning)
- [ ] Same day: blast tester recruitment posts (need supply for new demand)
- [ ] Continue 10 DMs/day to founders on X
- [ ] Engage every PH comment personally
- [ ] Post results/learnings on X (build-in-public content)

## Week 3: Double Down (April 15-21)
**Goal: 30 more sign-ups (cumulative 80)**

- [ ] Whatever worked in weeks 1-2, do 2x more of it
- [ ] Whatever didn't work, drop it completely
- [ ] If tests are actually completing: share real results/screenshots
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (different audience than X/Reddit)
- [ ] Consider referral: "Invite a friend, both get $5 credit" (only if real tests are happening)

## Week 4: Push to 100 (April 22-30)
**Goal: 20 more sign-ups (hit 100)**

- [ ] New round of community posts (fresh angle, share learnings)
- [ ] Email follow-up to sign-ups who haven't activated
- [ ] Second TikTok/content piece with real traction numbers
- [ ] Review what channels delivered, plan month 2

## Key Metrics (keep it simple)
- Total sign-ups (check Neon DB)
- Jobs posted (are founders actually paying?)
- Tests completed (is the marketplace working?)
- Vercel Analytics for traffic sources

## What NOT to Do in Month 1
- No Google/Facebook/LinkedIn ads (waste of money at this scale)
- No blog SEO (takes months, yields nothing now)
- No Mixpanel/Amplitude setup (Vercel Analytics is enough)
- No partnerships/guest posts (too slow)
- No webinars (you're 18 and solo, not hosting webinars)
- No building features — the product is done, focus on users

## DM Templates
